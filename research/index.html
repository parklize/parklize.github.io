Research

You can find my Google Scholar profile here.

My PhD topic is about "Semantics-Aware User Modeling and Recommender Systems in Online Social Networks". I'm open for collaboration on the following research topics:

  • Applied Deep Learning for Text Mining, Recommender Systems
  • Semantics-Aware Recommender Systems with Knowledge Graphs
  • User Modeling for Inferrring User Interest Profiles in Online Social Networks
