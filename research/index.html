Research

You can find my Google Scholar profile here. My current research interests include following topics, and I'm open for collaboration or advising prospective students on the following research topics:

  • User modeling on the Web (e.g., social media or MOOC platforms) to understand user behavior and characteristics (e.g., mining user interests)
  • Semantics-Aware Recommender Systems with Knowledge Graphs
  • Applied Deep Learning for Text Mining, Recommender Systems
  • Mining open Knowedge Grpah/Base such as Wikipedia, DBpedia, and Wikidata
  • AIOps (e.g., anomaly detection, time series forecasting)
  • Data science challenges (e.g., CIKM Analyticup and Semantic Web Challenges)
